My primary interests are computer vision and machine learning and their applicability to cell and microbiology. I’m also interested in computer architecture and software engineering.

I’m a member of the Anne Carpenter Laboratory, the Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard’s Imaging Platform, and a Chan Zuckerberg Initiative Engineering Fellow.

I co-maintain, alongside David Stirling, CellProfiler, an application for processing and analyzing microscopy images. Likewise, I co-maintain, alongside Shantanu Singh, Cytominer, a library for mining patterns in perturbation data. Most recently, I've been writing, alongside Alice Lucas, Piximi, an image understanding application.
